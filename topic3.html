Medical computer's topics about MRI and CT
MRI is an abbreviation for magnetic resonance imaging.
It is an indispensable medical device in current medical care.
The biggest merit of MRI is that it does not irradiate like CT or x-rays.
But when we enter the MRI room we have to be very cautious
because MRI utilizes a very strong magnetic force.

CT is an abbrebiation for computed tomography.
CT is also an indispensable medical device in current medical care.
The biggest merit of CT is its speed.
Recent CT can scan the entire body in about 10 seconds.
However, the disadvantage of CT is that it receives medical exposure.
